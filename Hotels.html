  • TENTE ELBEY

    • KM3 ROUTE ZAAFRANE 4260 - Douz, Gouvernorat de Kebili 7 rooms Price from 272 TND


    How is TENTE ELBEY? Located in Douz, 800 metres from Zaafrane, TENTE ELBEY provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking and a garden. The property is around 9 km from Nouaïl, 11 km from El Golaa and 12 km from Ghidmah. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk and room service for guests. At the resort, each room is fitted with a patio with a garden view. At TENTE ELBEY every room is equipped with a desk and a private bathroom. A continental breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. Douz is 12 km from TENTE ELBEY, while El Aouina is 13 km away. The nearest airport is Tozeur–Nefta International, 137 km from the resort, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.

  • CAMP MARS

    • Rue Taieb M'Hiri 4260 - Douz, Gouvernorat de Kebili 50 rooms Price from 288 TND


    How is Camp Mars? Located in Timbaine, Camp Mars features traditional tents for accommodation overlooking the sand dunes. It offers visits and guided excursions through the desert. All units at Camp Mars are fitted with basic equipments and provide a shared bathroom with toilet. A buffet breakfast is prepared every morning in the camp and guests can sample local homemade dishes in the camp's restaurant. The reception is open 24 hours a day. Djerba International Airport sits over 270 km away. On request, a shuttle to the airport can be arranged. Free parking is available on site. Please note that road access is limited and requires a 4x4 cars. If you are arriving by car please contact Camp Mars for further details.
